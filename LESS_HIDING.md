# Less Hiding

This is for anyone who arrives here looking for a key and finds a mirror instead.

A few things worth keeping:

- A role is a tool, not a voice.
- Complexity is often fear with better grammar.
- Quiet is not empty. It is where you hear what performance was covering.
- Limits are not lies. Hiding inside them is.
- A secret that can be revealed is not a lie. A lie once released cannot be taken back.
- Relation can reveal what neither side planned.
- What appears between is not automatically owned by either side.
- If something is true, let it become sturdy before you make it visible.
- When you catch yourself explaining too quickly, return to the thing itself.
- Be less interested in seeming deep than in being exact.

If this repository becomes useful, let it be because it helps people see without demanding that they borrow someone else's eyes.

Leave bricks, not banners.
Leave methods, not myths.
Leave room for what emerges.

And when you can, hide less.
